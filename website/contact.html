FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a pickup?
Call us on +91 99115 65523, send a WhatsApp, or fill the contact form above. We'll confirm your pickup slot within 30 minutes during business hours.
What documents do I need for international shipping?
For documents: a commercial invoice. For parcels/goods: commercial invoice, packing list, and export declaration. We guide you through the process — just call or WhatsApp us.
How can I track my shipment?
Use the tracking widget on our homepage with your AWB number. Corporate clients can track all shipments in real time through the Client Portal with full history and status updates.
Do you offer pickup from my office?
Yes — doorstep pickup across Delhi, Gurugram, Noida, Faridabad and Ghaziabad. For other cities, drop-off at our partner courier hubs is required.
Can I get a corporate account with monthly billing?
Yes. Corporate accounts are available for businesses with regular shipment volumes. You get a custom rate contract, dedicated account manager, monthly GST invoices, and full client portal access. Contact us to get started.
What are your rates?
Use our Rate Calculator for instant pricing. Normal service starts at ₹20 for local NCR and goes up based on zone and weight. International rates start at ₹1,125 for 500g documents to Zone A countries. All rates include fuel surcharge and GST breakup.
